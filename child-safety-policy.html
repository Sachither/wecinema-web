Child Safety Standards

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

At WeCinema Player, developed by Luther Sabastine, the safety of children is our absolute priority. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and any form of child exploitation or abuse (CSAE).

1. Prohibited Content and Behavior

Users are strictly prohibited from using WeCinema Player to store, share, synchronize, transmit, or promote any content that depicts:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) or Imagery (CSAE).
  • Child nudity or sexualization of minors.
  • Promotion of child exploitation or trafficking.

2. Monitoring and Zero Tolerance

We employ a combination of proactive measures and community reporting to identify violations:

Permanent Bans: Any account found to be involved in the distribution, viewing, or promotion of prohibited content will be immediately and permanently banned without warning.

Device Blacklisting: In severe cases, we reserve the right to blacklist devices from accessing our services to prevent recidivism.

3. Reporting Mechanisms

We empower our community to act as a first line of defense. If you encounter prohibited content or have safety concerns, please utilize the following channels:

smartphone In-App Reporting

Users can report any participant directly within a watch room or use the "Report a Concern" feature in the App Settings.

4. Cooperation with Law Enforcement

WeCinema Player is committed to fulfilling our legal obligations under international and local laws:

  • NCMEC Reporting: We will report all identified instances of CSAM to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies globally.
  • Data Preservation: We will preserve relevant data to assist in the investigation and prosecution of individuals involved in child exploitation.
