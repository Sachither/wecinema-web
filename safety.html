🛡️ 5. Child Safety & Prohibited Content Standards

WeCinema, developed by Luther Sabastine, maintains a Strict Zero-Tolerance Policy regarding Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). We are legally committed to maintaining a safe digital environment and protecting minors from all forms of exploitation.

✔

Prohibited Content Standards

Users are strictly forbidden from using WeCinema to host, share, synchronize, or transmit any imagery or data that depicts the sexual abuse or exploitation of children. This includes, but is not limited to, Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM) and the sexualization of minors.

✔

Immediate Enforcement & Accountability

Any account identified as sharing or promoting such content will be permanently banned without warning. We reserve the right to ban device IDs and IP addresses to prevent further access to our services.

✔

Mandatory In-App Reporting Tools

In compliance with Google Play standards, WeCinema includes built-in reporting tools. Users can flag participants or specific rooms directly from the "Watch Room" interface or via the "Safety Center" in settings.

✉

Official Reporting Channel

All reports are reviewed within 24 hours with the highest priority. If you encounter illegal content, report it via the app or contact our Child Safety Lead immediately:
